# Full transaction transparency to all Members

Every Member can see every Deposit and every spend in a Pool (merchant, amount, who paid/was reimbursed) in a shared, real-time ledger — not just the Organizer.

This is the direct counterweight to single-Organizer spending authority ([0004](./0004-single-organizer-authority.md)): since v1 deliberately has no approval workflow, transparency is the remaining mechanism that makes trusting one person with pooled money acceptable. It also underwrites the pro-rata refund on Closure ([0006](./0006-pool-closure-and-refund.md)) — Members can verify the math themselves rather than taking the Organizer's word for it.
