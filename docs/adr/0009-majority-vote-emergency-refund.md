# Majority-vote emergency refund as the sole check on Organizer authority

Single-Organizer spending authority ([0004](./0004-single-organizer-authority.md)) means Members must trust one person with pooled money, with no day-to-day approval workflow. To bound the risk (an unresponsive or untrustworthy Organizer), non-Organizer Members can trigger an emergency Closure by **simple majority vote** (>50% of non-Organizer Members) — unanimous was rejected because one silent/unresponsive Member could block it, defeating the point of a safety valve; a quorum/timeout system was rejected as more machinery than v1 needs.

Voting is **one-Member-one-vote**, not weighted by Deposit size — this matters most in Open Pools, where contributions are unequal by design. Weighted voting was rejected as unnecessary complexity (computing a "majority of weighted shares" is a much fuzzier threshold than counting heads). Fairness with respect to money-at-stake is instead handled entirely by the pro-rata refund itself ([0006](./0006-pool-closure-and-refund.md)) — someone who deposited more simply gets more back when the vote succeeds, regardless of how the vote was decided.

A successful vote **immediately Closes the Pool**, reusing the existing Closure mechanism ([0006](./0006-pool-closure-and-refund.md)) — remaining balance is refunded pro-rata to all Members. This inherently cannot claw back money the Organizer already legitimately spent before the vote; it only protects whatever remains.

We considered and rejected a second, independent safety mechanism: capping the Organizer's spending to only the amount they personally deposited (rather than the full Pool balance). This was rejected because (a) it's redundant with the vote-refund mechanism above, and (b) it breaks the core use case of the Organizer paying a single lump sum (e.g. the group's Airbnb) out of everyone's pooled money — if the Organizer can only spend their own share, nobody has authority to spend the rest. Single-Organizer authority over the *full* Pool balance stands; the vote is the only check on it.
